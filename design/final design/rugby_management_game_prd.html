# Rugby Management Simulation Game - UI/UX Design PRD ## Project Overview A modern, web-based (React) rugby management simulation game where players take on the role of a club manager. ## Design Goals * **Modern Aesthetic:** Clean lines, dark mode support, and high-quality typography. * **Data-Rich but Accessible:** Presenting complex stats clearly using charts and structured grids. * **Intuitive UX:** Logical navigation and clear feedback for management actions. ## Proposed Screens ### 1. Dashboard (Home) * **Purpose:** Central hub for the manager. * **Key Features:** * Club Overview (Name, Logo, League Position). * Recent Form (last 5 matches). * Next Match Preview (Opponent, Venue, Countdown). * Quick links to Squad and Tactics. * News Feed/Notifications. ### 2. Squad Management * **Purpose:** Deep dive into the roster. * **Key Features:** * Sortable/Filterable Player Table (Name, Age, Position, Rating, Value, Fitness). * Individual Player Card (detailed attributes: Strength, Speed, Handling, Kicking, etc.). * Contract Status and Salary info. ### 3. Tactics & Lineup * **Purpose:** Preparing for the next match. * **Key Features:** * Interactive Pitch View (Drag-and-drop players into positions). * Formation Selector (e.g., standard rugby union setups). * Team Instructions (Attacking style, Defensive line, Set-piece focus). * Bench/Substitutes management. ### 4. Match Center * **Purpose:** Experiencing the match simulation. * **Key Features:** * Live Scoreboard & Clock. * 2D/3D Visual representation or detailed Text Commentary. * Real-time Match Stats (Possession, Territory, Penalties, Tackles). * In-game Management (Substitutions, Tactical shifts). ### 5. Transfer Market & Scouting * **Purpose:** Growing the club. * **Key Features:** * Global Player Search. * Scouting Reports. * Transfer Negotiation Interface. ## Visual Direction * **Theme:** Dark mode primarily (sleek, professional sports feel). * **Primary Color:** Deep Navy or Emerald Green (Rugby associations). * **Accent Color:** High-contrast Gold or Electric Blue for CTAs. * **Typography:** Robust sans-serif (e.g., Inter, Montserrat).
